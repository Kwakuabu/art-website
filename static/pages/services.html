Our Work

A sample of what we have delivered.

Organisational Research 5 weeks · Quantitative

Mobile Money Adoption and SME Growth in Greater Accra

A quantitative study examining how mobile payment platforms changed operations across 220 small enterprises in Accra, La Dade Kotopon, and Tema.

Academic Research 8 weeks · Mixed Methods

Digital Financial Inclusion in Rural Volta Region

A mixed-methods undergraduate dissertation exploring barriers to digital banking adoption among rural communities across four districts.

Development Research 6 weeks · Pre-Post Design

Nutrition Intervention Impact Assessment, Northern Region

An impact evaluation for an NGO running a community nutrition programme across 12 communities.

Business Research 3 weeks · Survey & Desk

Agri-Tech Market Sizing for Smallholder Farmer Platform

A feasibility and market sizing study including competitor mapping, willingness-to-pay analysis, and demand projections.

Academic Research MSc Thesis · Machine Learning

A Machine Learning Framework for Threat Detection in Electronic Medical Records Systems

Design science study validating Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM, and MLP against EMR access logs from 37 Military Hospital. Random Forest and XGBoost exceeded 99% F1-scores on real-world validation.

Academic Research BRSET · Differential Privacy

Privacy-Utility-Fairness Trilemma in Ophthalmological Machine Learning

Differential privacy evaluation on 14,280 retinal records from BRSET. ε=2.0 with Laplace achieved clinically viable utility (MLP macro F1=0.6957, AUC=0.9320).

About Us

Ghana's Research Partner.

ART Ghana (Academy of Research and Technology) connects organisations and students with Ghana's best research talent, backed by a rigorous quality assurance process and an expert academic advisory team.

flag

Our Mission

To make credible, high-quality research accessible to every organisation and individual in Ghana, and to give Ghana's emerging research talent a professional platform to thrive.

visibility

Our Vision

A Ghana where research drives every major decision, in business, in policy, and in academia, and where Ghanaian researchers are recognised globally for the rigour and relevance of their work.

verified

Our Standard

Every deliverable passes a three-layer quality check: plagiarism detection, originality screening, and a senior quality reviewer, before it reaches the client.

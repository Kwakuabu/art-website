The Process

Simple from start to finish.

Whether you are an NGO commissioning a programme evaluation or a student completing your dissertation, the process is the same. Clear, trackable, and built around your deadline.

01
Tell us what you need

Describe your research project: topic, scope, deadline, and any specific requirements. Takes under five minutes to submit.

02
We match you with the right team

We surface consultants whose academic background and domain expertise fit your project. You choose who you work with.

03
Track progress in real time

Work directly with your consultant. Share documents, give feedback, and track progress in real time through our platform.

04
Receive and approve your research

Get quality-checked research you can present with confidence. On time, within budget, ready to use.
